Outline

Explore the core elements of successful project management, from initiation and planning through execution and closing. We start with initiation, establishing the 'why' and basic outline of your project. Then, we move into detailed planning, where you'll learn to define scope, build schedules, manage resources, and engage stakeholders. The execution phase focuses on bringing plans to life, guiding your team, and managing progress. Finally, we cover project closing, ensuring all work is finalized and valuable lessons are captured for the future.

Session 1: Introduction to Project Management

  • Fundamental elements of project management
  • Project phases
  • Planning areas
  • Framework for successfully managing projects

Session 2: Scope, Stakeholders, and Communications

  • Project initiation and scope
  • Inclusive approach to stakeholder management
  • Intentional communication techniques
  • Team motivation and engagement

Session 3: Deliverables, Schedule, and Budgets

  • Clearly defining project scope and objectives
  • Developing actionable deliverables and ensuring quality
  • Creating realistic project schedules and managing tasks
  • Strategies for keeping projects on track and within scope

Session 4: Project Risks and Closing

  • Project Risk and Challenges
  • Forward-thinking project closing activities
  • Agile techniques
  • Review of project management strategies

Key Tools & Resources Included

PIP Design Checklist

A printable, step-by-step checklist ensuring users include all necessary components for a practical, measurable, and legally defensible Performance Improvement Plan, directly drawn from course best practices.

Discipline Documentation Dos & Don'ts

A concise, one-page guide summarizing critical points for documenting disciplinary actions to maintain compliance and reduce risk, based on legal pitfalls discussed in the course.

"Difficult Conversation" Prep Worksheet

A simple, fillable template helping managers organize their thoughts and key talking points before conducting a performance or disciplinary meeting, reflecting strategies taught in the course.

The Compliant PIP Process Flowchart

A visual overview mapping the key stages and decision points in implementing a PIP, from initial warning to conclusion, as outlined in the course.

Top 5 Legal Mistakes in Employee Discipline (and How to Avoid Them)

A quick tip sheet highlighting common compliance errors managers make (drawn from course warnings) and offering brief solutions.

Sample Phrases for Performance Feedback

A short list providing examples of constructive and legally sound language snippets for use in PIPs or performance discussions, based on course examples (ensure these are generalized).
